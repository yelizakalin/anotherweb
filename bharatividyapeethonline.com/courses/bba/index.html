Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

  • Instructor BVDU Tech
  • 0 Students Enrolled

  • Price ₹75,000.00
  • Overview
  • Curriculum
  • Instructor

This 3-year program offered by Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University (BVDU) helps students excel in today’s business market. With the vision of “Social Transformation Through Dynamic Education” this program strives to build leaders with exceptional business knowledge & problem-solving skills. Participants can expect to gain an in-depth understanding of the principles, theories, and functional areas of businesses through an interactive online learning experience.

BBA is a 36-month, 132 credit hour program with distinct 35 courses. Taught by industry experts, it is an online program which uses a variety of teaching and learning methods such as interactive lectures, assignments, case studies and discussion forums. These learning modes facilitate the exchange of innovative ideas which enable high collaboration levels among the students.

 

Objectives:

  1. To provide students with an in-depth knowledge of Management and Business concepts
  2. To provide students with a firm foundation in both theoretical and practical concepts and applications to meet the various needs of business organisations at a global level
  3. To prepare students for the challenging responsibilities and career opportunities with Organizations and as Business Leaders.
